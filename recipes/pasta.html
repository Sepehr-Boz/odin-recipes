Pasta Salad

Description

  • pasta
  • cherry tomatoes
  • salami
  • pepperoni
  • bell pepper
  • black olives
  • jar pimentos
  • Italian salad dressing
  1. Get all ingredients ready
  2. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta until firm ( ~12 minutes )
  3. Combine the pasta with tomatoes, cheese, salami, pepperoni, green pepper, olives, and pimentos. Pour in salad dressing.
  4. Eat :)
